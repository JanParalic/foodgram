This project is a part of our fourth semesters grade in WAD at UofG. The idea was to create a web page similar in style to the very popular social network; Instagram. The main feature of our platform was to create the content solely about food and give people a more objective way of rating pictures. There are three main rating scales: HEALTH, COOKING and STYLE. One is to show how hard was if to prepare that meal, second was to show how good of a photo it is so that we have a distinction between good photographs and good chefs and lastly one scale to determine the healthiness of the meal. Via the HEALTH scale we aim to create a bit of peer pressure to get people eating more healthy foods. If you like this idea, feel free to contribute with your own pictures.
